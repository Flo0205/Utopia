Welcome to Utopia

Utopia is a software that adds plugin support to the game "SCP: Secret Laboratory".
It's based on Atlas (Copyright 2019 Luke Janik, MIT License).

The goal of Utopia is to at simple accessibility to the full potential of Atlas and make that as easy and effective as possieble.
Also Utopia patches some security and performance issues.

For further support join our Discord.
There you can suggest new features and improvments, report bugs and get help with your plugin.
